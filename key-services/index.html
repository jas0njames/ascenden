KEY SERVICES

Our Unique Approach

Why are organizations struggling so much with optimizing their use of systems? Is something being overlooked? We believe so!

Ascenden has specialized in helping organizations through systems implementations since 1998. While conventional wisdom has focused on technology, computer science, and methodology, we have recognized that significant inhibitors are being overlooked and allowed to perpetuate, preventing organizations from optimizing and realizing full benefits.

These inhibitors appear to be simple and inconsequential, but we have consistently found them at the root of organizational problems.

These factors include:

  • Change Management
  • Governance
  • Communication
  • Ownership of workflow process reengineering
  • Operational culture change

Our approach is to start in the operational trenches and then ascend four steps that focus on the impact of human factors in systems implementations. During the ascent we record significant observations that are not commonly captured. We apply highly experienced analysis and interpretation resulting in the discovery of key inhibitors and opportunities that have been overlooked. Actionable recommendations are designed to address the key inhibitors and opportunities, using a holistic approach to ensure that current state dynamics are addressed and that responses to change are anticipated. This results in recommendations that are realistic, sustainable, and scalable.

Our Services

Implementing complex systems involves much more than the application of science and methodology. That's because it involves people and change.

Ascenden's professional services are extraordinary in that they are always executed with a bifocal approach. Services are delivered in a way that achieves measurable results beyond excellence and at the same time, Ascenden consultants are functioning as trained change agents, working to model, tutor, and help client employees adapt, embrace, and ultimately advocate for sustainable movement towards better practice.
