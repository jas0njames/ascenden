Augmentation
Ultimate augmentation occurs when we get in the trenches and work alongside your staff with a focus not just on getting the work done but also on discovering and understanding the dynamics at play among your people, their workflow processes and the interrelationships.
Assessment
Ultimate assessment occurs when key assessment data is combined with augmentation data to provide fully contextualized data, rich in significant observations that are not commonly captured. This is followed by highly experienced analysis and interpretation resulting in discovery of key inhibitors and opportunities that have previously escaped attention.
Remodel
Ultimate remodel occurs when processes and structures are carefully re‑engineered to address the key inhibitors and opportunities discovered in the assessment, using a holistic approach to ensure that all current state dynamics are addressed and that responses to change are anticipated, resulting in a model that is realistic, sustainable, and scalable.
Implementation
Ultimate implementation requires the same strategy as ultimate assessment. Sustainable change is not achieved by management directive accompanied by new SOP. It occurs when strong and communicative leadership is accompanied by the efforts of trained change agents working in augmentation roles who are modeling, tutoring, and helping employees to adapt, embrace, and ultimately advocate for the new model. This "life-on-life" component has become a lost art in modern business culture. Though simple in concept, it is extremely difficult to master.
